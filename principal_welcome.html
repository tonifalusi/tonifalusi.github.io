Welcome from our Principal

Emmaus Christian School is a beautiful small school in the heart of one of Canberra’s most established suburbs.

At our school, Christ is the centre of all that we believe, teach and learn. The gospel of Jesus Christ is not an add-on to the education and nurture that we provide; it is integrated and embedded in all that we do. Whilst many schools have Christian origins and values, at Emmaus we believe that the gospel is at the heart of the curriculum and central to all teaching and learning.

The Christian view of the world that we operate from is one based on biblical truths and recognition that Jesus is Lord and King over all of life.

We furthermore believe that Christian education at Emmaus is not a ‘fee-for-service’ or a transaction as such but rather an engaging partnership between home and school. It is therefore essential that parents subscribe to and agree with the ethos of the School.

As all families today face challenges, Emmaus Christian School wishes to come alongside our families and support, affirm and magnify all that determines a gospel-shaped community. Christian education at Emmaus includes an open and transparent challenge for all staff and students to reach their goals and so bring glory to our living God.

Staff at our school take seriously their duty to provide a high quality teaching and learning program – one that is seasoned and balanced with a warm and engaging pastoral care program. The consistent high standards of academic results and family-like school culture are evidence of the care and commitment of staff.

I hope that this website will provide you with the information you need and I look forward to a time meeting you and your family. In the meantime, if you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to get in touch with our Enrolment Officer.

Yours in Christ,

Erik Hofsink (Principal)
